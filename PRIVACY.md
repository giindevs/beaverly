# Privacy Policy — Beaverly Shelf

**Last updated:** 19 July 2026
🌐 [Versão em português](PRIVACY.pt-BR.md)

**Beaverly Shelf** is a desktop app for organizing your e-book library and reading your
books. It is built on a simple principle: **it works 100% offline and does not collect
your data.** This policy explains, plainly, what the app does — and does not do — with
information.

In short: **we do not collect, sell, or share your data. Nothing leaves your computer
automatically.**

---

## 1. Who we are

Beaverly ("we"). Contact: **contato@beaverly.com.br**.

## 2. Data we collect

**None, automatically.** Beaverly Shelf has **no** telemetry, **no** analytics, shows
**no** ads, and does **not** track you. No sign-up is required and we build no profile
about you.

## 3. Data that stays on your device (which we never see)

Everything you do in the app is saved **locally, on your computer**:

- your library (the book files and the folders you choose);
- covers, metadata and your annotations (highlights and notes);
- app settings (e.g. language and theme), stored under `~/.shelf`
  (`%USERPROFILE%\.shelf` on Windows).

This data is **not** sent to us or anyone else. You control it and can delete it at any
time.

## 4. Online features — only when YOU choose

Some optional features use the internet, and **only when you click them**. In no case do
we send your book files:

- **Fetch covers/metadata (optional):** when you ask, the app sends **only the search
  text** (title, author or ISBN) to catalog services (e.g. Open Library, Google Books,
  and Beaverly's public aggregator). Your files are never sent, and you confirm before
  anything is saved.
- **"Search the web" (in the reader):** opens your default browser with the selected
  term (e.g. DuckDuckGo). From there, the search engine's/browser's policy applies.
- **Add-ons (optional):** if you install an add-on, queries go to the add-on URL **you**
  configured. Add-ons are third-party services, run by whoever provides them.
- **Beaverly account (optional):** you may optionally connect a Beaverly account to sync
  or fetch information. It is entirely optional; the app works fully without one.
- **Send to your e-reader by e-mail (optional):** if you configure your own e-mail
  (SMTP) server, the app uses **your** credentials to e-mail a book. Those credentials
  are stored **encrypted on your computer** and are never sent to us.

## 5. Third parties

When you use an online feature above, data goes directly to the service you chose
(metadata provider, search engine, add-on, your e-mail provider, or the Beaverly
platform). Each has its own privacy policy. We do not intermediate or store that traffic.

## 6. Security

Local secrets (such as your e-mail password or a connected account's token) are stored
**encrypted** on your device (DPAPI on Windows; AES-256-GCM with a restricted-permission
key file on Linux) and are never written in plain text or to logs.

## 7. Children

Beaverly Shelf is general-audience software and is not directed at children. Since we
collect no personal data, we collect no data from children.

## 8. Your rights

Because your data lives on your device, **you have full control**: you can view, export
(back up) or delete it whenever you want. Uninstalling the app does not delete your
libraries (they remain in the folders you chose); you remove those manually if you wish.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top marks the current version;
significant changes will be announced on Beaverly's official channels.

## 10. Contact

Questions about privacy? Write to **contato@beaverly.com.br**.
