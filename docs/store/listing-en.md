## Description
Beaverly Shelf is a calm, local-first desktop app for readers. It keeps your entire e-book library on your own computer and lets you read your books — PDF, EPUB and CBZ — right inside the app, with highlights and notes.

No account. No sign-up. No telemetry. Beaverly Shelf works 100% offline; the only things that ever reach the internet are actions you explicitly choose, such as optionally fetching a cover.

What you can do:

• Build a local library — import your e-books (files or folders) and organize them by author, series, tags and collections.
• Read inside the app — a clean reading mode for PDF, EPUB and CBZ, with a table of contents, text selection and a browser-style selection toolbar (copy, highlight, note, search the web).
• Highlight and take notes — annotate as you read. Your original files are never modified.
• Covers and details (optional) — fetch covers, synopses and author info online when you want. Only the search text is sent, never your files, and you confirm before anything is saved.
• Back up your library — to a folder you choose, such as a synced cloud folder. The app never uploads anything by itself.
• Read in your language — Portuguese, English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese and Chinese.

Privacy first: your library, your reading and your notes stay on your machine. A Beaverly account is optional and never required.

Beaverly Shelf is part of the wider Beaverly reading ecosystem — learn more at beaverly.com.br.

## Product features
- Local e-book library: organize by author, series, tags and collections
- Built-in reader for PDF, EPUB and CBZ
- Highlights and notes — your original files are never changed
- Clean reading mode with table of contents and text selection
- Optional online covers and metadata (opt-in, privacy-friendly)
- Back up your library to any folder you choose
- Interface in 8 languages
- 100% offline — no account, no telemetry
